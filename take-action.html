Call your representative
Main Senate switchboard
202-224-3121
Script to use:
Hi, my name is [your name] and I'm a constituent from [your city, state]. I'm calling to urge [Senator's name] to co-sponsor the AI in Hiring Transparency Act. Workers deserve to know when AI is making decisions about their job applications, and they need the right to request a human review. This is a basic transparency measure. I'd love to know the senator's position. Thank you for your time.
Tips: Be brief and polite. The person answering is not the Senator — they're just taking messages. One call from a constituent matters.
